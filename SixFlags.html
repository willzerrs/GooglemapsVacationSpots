Six Flags Magic Mountain

Six Flags Magic Mountain is a 262-acre theme park located in the Valencia neighborhood of Santa Clarita, California, north of Los Angeles. It opened on May 30, 1971, as Magic Mountain, a development of the Newhall Land and Farming Company.
